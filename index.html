Hit the target


Rules of the game

The target moves in different directions. Your task is to earn points by hitting the target.

Yellow color gives you 5 points. Red color gives you 3 points. Blue color gives you 2 points. Black color gives you 1 point.

For every 25 points you move to the next level.

If you miss the target, you lose one life. You will only have 3 lives.

Good luck!
